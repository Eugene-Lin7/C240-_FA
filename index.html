Swimming Basics for Beginners

Water Safety

Always swim in designated areas with lifeguards. Never swim alone and always wear appropriate gear.

Breathing Techniques

Master the art of controlled breathing. Inhale above water and exhale below for efficient swimming.

Body Position

Keep your body horizontal and streamlined in the water. This reduces drag and improves efficiency.

Floating

Learn to float effortlessly. This is fundamental for survival and builds confidence in water.
